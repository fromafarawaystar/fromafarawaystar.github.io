## Claire Zadowsky

A streetwise survivor with a sharp mind and a hidden heart, Claire Zadowsky knows how to fight, adapt, and bargain her way through any world—whether it’s the alleys of St. Louis or the unknowns of first contact. 

---

## Zhara nar Korith

Curious and bold, Zhara nar Korith is a young Kilrathi eager to explore new worlds. Torn between family honor and her own questions, she is determined to understand—and shape—the future unfolding around her.

---

## Kythara nar Korith

A seasoned engineer and devoted mother, Kythara nar Korith balances Kilrathi tradition with the demands of a new world. Sharp, disciplined, and fiercely protective, she steers her clan through uncertain times.

---

## Caterina Rossi

Resourceful and quick-witted, Caterina Rossi grew up on the streets of Genoa. Fiercely independent and adaptable, she finds herself caught between worlds as the city’s tides begin to shift.

---

## Kharal nar Stagash

Young and earnest, Kharal nar Stagash is a Kilrathi soldier from the deep caves of his home planet. Thrust into a confusing new world, he’s determined to prove himself—though his greatest tests may come from the heart.

---

## Deyah nar Stagash

Proud and strong-willed, Deyah nar Stagash upholds her clan’s traditions as both mother and matriarch. With wisdom and quiet resolve, she weaves the destinies of those she loves, even as new challenges arise.
